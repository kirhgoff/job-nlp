.Net Developers - Junior to Mid Senior
QuantumIT - Melbourne VIC

QuantumIT is a very fast growing and highly successful Melbourne based consultancy and software products company with a substantial .NET practice building operational solutions using C#, ASP.NET, ORM (Nhibernate and Entity Framework), MVC, SQL Server .
We have a fast expanding global footprint and a team of highly motivated and dedicated software professionals.
We now need additional full stack Microsoft .NET Developers to work across new and existing lines of business that will call upon your skills to work in:

  • UI and/or back end
  • an Agile environment
  • a TDD (Test Driven Development) and continuous integration process
  • multiple projects (both consulting and product development )

Our .NET practice has a substantial ongoing demand arising from the continued success of our Student Placement Product line ( www.inplace.com.au / www.inplacenetwork.com ) and consulting work generated from long standing relationships with Tier 1 / Tier 2 corporate clients.
We are seeking developers who are adaptable and enjoy a range of delivery challenges.We require:

  • 3+ Years experience with C#
  • 3+ Years experience with ASP.NET MVC
  • 3+ Years experience with Javascript
  • Working Knowledge of RESTful services
  • Strong MS SQL Server/T-SQL skills
  • Strong TDD approach to development
  • Self-Motivated and Problem Solver

Preferably you will also have:

  • 2+ Years experience with Angular JS
  • Knowledge of TypeScript
  • Experience with NHibernate, Entity Framework
  • Experience with GIT
  • Experience with HTML5 and Responsive Design

We look for people who thrive in outcomes based project work and who enjoy working in fast paced delivery environments.
Your reward will be to work across a variety of exciting products that are currently spearheading Industry transformations through Information Technology.
Our head office is on the north eastern fringes of the CBD. We are a technology company of around 100 people and have operated in Melbourne continuously for the last 25 years.

Job Type: Full-time
